About The Event

Karmatek is the annual technolgical fest of Govenment College of Engineering & Ceramic Technology. It is organised around the time of the onset of every even semester. Karmatek is all about knowledge diffusion with added thrills. Students from GCECT as well as various other colleges awaits each year to exhibit their talents.

Where

GCECT, KOLKATA

When

Monday to Wednesday
10-12 December
